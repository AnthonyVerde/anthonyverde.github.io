Web Design with Compassion Canada

I worked closely with the digital experience team at Compassion Canada to design and build pages for their website. From updating existing pages to building out campaign promo sections, I was involved in every step of the way.

  • Role: Freelance Web Designer
  • Tools: Figma, Wordpress, Visual Studio Code, Sourcetree, Codekit, Adobe CC (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop)

Updating Exisiting Pages

There were a number of pages and sections on Compassion's website that were outdated and required much-needed visual and content changes. Working with wireframes provided by the UX team, I would create hi-fi designs and provide recommendations around visuals and layout as needed. Once approved, I would translate designs into code (using HTML5, CSS3, JS, jQuery, PHP).

Homepage Takeover

Significant campaigns required updating Compassion's homepage to drive traffic to the campaign pages.

Financial Impact Reports

Compassion's financial impact section required updating on an annual basis. It was important to present the information in a visual way that keeps visitors interested and able to digest the financial figures and data.

This website was proudly designed and built by a living, breathing human being.
