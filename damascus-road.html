New Website for The Damascus Road Foundation

The Damascus Road Foundation recently rebranded and needed a new website designed and built. I worked alongside Story Digital Marketing's team to take on the task of scrapping the old website and creating a new website from the ground up using the exciting new brand.

  • Role: Design and Development Lead
  • Tools: Figma, Wordpress, Elementor
  • Project Management: Story Digital Marketing

The Process

Roughed out some layouts of the homepage on pen and paper.

Dialed in the layout at the wireframe stage in Figma.

Introduced the new brand and photography.

Moved into Wordpress and began building the approved designs in Elementor.

This website was proudly designed and built by a living, breathing human being.
