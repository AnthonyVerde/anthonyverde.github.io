Design System Contribution: Data Visualization

I was tasked to research and develop on-brand and accessible data visualization for Canada Life and define rules and guidelines around its use within the design system. I researched many other successful brands' design systems and data visualization styles and rules for inspiration and guidance. I also collaborated with accessibility experts to build a colour palette that meets the acceptable contrast ratios.

Illustration & Iconography

I regularly contribute to our ever-growing continuous line style iconography and illustration library for use across Canada Life's digital properties. The icons are designed to create visual interest where a photo may not be the best option and are usually accompanied with content or a CTA. Like the icons, the illustrations are used in place of a photo, usually in a charmer or displayed on a 404 error page.
