Meet the designer

My name's Anthony Green — you can call me Tony.

I'm a designer based in Ontario, Canada. I've always been passionate about all things creative — ever since I got my first set of crayons.

Throughout my career, I've had the pleasure of working with some amazing companies and individuals allowing me to be involved in some exciting projects and build up a wide range of skills.

Outside of my design life, I love spending time with my wonderful wife and two amazing children (two-and-a-half if you count the labradoodle). We love the great outdoors. Whether it's a short hike outside the city or a canoe trip in beautiful Muskoka, we take any opportunity—rain or shine—to get some fresh air.

I'm a big fan of almost anything with wheels, I appreciate a good cup of coffee, I love to laugh, and I enjoy working with my hands.

If you'd like to collaborate, let's chat!
