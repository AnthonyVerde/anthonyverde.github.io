Meet the designer

More than just a guy who creates stuff.

“If what you’re doing doesn’t make you happy, you’re doing the wrong thing.”

- Bob Ross

My name's Anthony Green — you can call me Tony.

I’m a multi-disciplined designer specializing in web design, branding, graphic design, and illustration. I have nearly 20 years of experience in the industry and have enjoyed many of those years as an in-house brand designer with some of the biggest insurance brands in Canada. I am well versed in creating hifi mockups, coding pixel-perfect pages, working with design systems, and designing engaging online content. I’m passionate about what I do, enjoy a good laugh, and I love collaborating with other creative folks.

When I’m not at my desk, you’ll find me outdoors with my wife and kids, usually getting our hands dirty in the garden or going for a hike; tinkering in the workshop; or building/fixing something around the house. I love to learn and I’m always up for new and exciting opportunities.

Give me a shout to see how we can work together!
