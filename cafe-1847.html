Café 1847

Café 1847 brand development

Brief

Lead the development and design of the Café 1847 brand. Café 1847 is a high-end, full-service coffee bar located exclusively at each of Canada Life's campuses.

I was tasked with working closely with Canada Life's brand and vendor services teams to come up with the name and logo design for the coffee bar. "Café 1847" was chosen as a nod to Canada Life's history and the company's founding date. The logo fits perfectly into Canada Life's strong and recognizable brand by sharing the iconic red square.
